Connect acquiring for your business

Internet acquiring is the acceptance of payment by bank cards (Visa, Mastercard, Prostir) and other payment methods on your website or application.

Thanks to connected acquiring, customers are able to make cashless purchases anywhere, 24/7.

The iPay.ua payment service, unlike a bank, works with several partner banks at once. If one of the banks is unavailable or rejects the transaction, it is instantly redirected to other banks until it is successfully completed.

This allows you to reduce the number of unsuccessful payments several times.
